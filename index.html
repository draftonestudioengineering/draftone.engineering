About us

DraftOne is a fully integrated technical office that turns design intent into clear, build-ready documentation. We specialise in Shop Drawings, As-Built Drawings, Coordination Models, and Quantity Take-Offs for construction projects across Saudi Arabia.

We work to your standards (title blocks, layering, naming conventions and submittal formats), maintain clean revision control and deliver complete packages (DWG + PDF + schedules). Our coordination process focuses on early clash detection, structured responses to consultant comments and fast turnarounds.

Capabilities by discipline

  • Architectural: shop details (partitions, doors/windows, RCP), elevations/sections, fit-out details, schedules and room data sheets.
  • Civil / Structural: dimensional control, rebar shop drawings & bending schedules, GA drawings, slab openings, embeds, sleeves and builder’s work.
  • Electrical: lighting & power layouts, SLDs, panel schedules, cable tray routing/sizing, earthing/LPS and low-current (data, CCTV, access, PA/BGM).
  • Mechanical: HVAC ducting and equipment layouts, schematics & schedules, plumbing & drainage with pipe sizing/slopes, firefighting networks and hydraulics.
  • Coordination & BIM: clash notes & tracking, combined coordination models (AutoCAD/Revit), builder’s work and sleeve/opening layouts.
  • QTO: measurable, auditable take-offs with Excel BOQs and assumptions log.
